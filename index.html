Ping Pong


Directions | Examples | GitHub

Directions

Enter a number from 1 to 3999, then click "Go!". The application will count up to the number that you entered, substituting the word "pong" for numbers divisible by three, the word "ping" for numbers divisible by five, and the words "ping pong" for numbers divisible by both three and five. This is a classis branching exercise intended to frustrate and teach students of code.

Examples

Input: 3

1 2 ping

Input: 5

1 2 ping 4 pong

Input: 15

1 2 ping 4 pong ping 7 8 ping pong 11 ping 13 14 ping pong
